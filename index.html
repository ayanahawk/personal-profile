Websites

Food Oasis was made for users to know where the nearest produce and farmers markets are in their zip code. For this app, I worked on managing the data of the different grocery stores and still managing new and upcoming farmer markets and community gardens. A new goal for this app is to be able to find local food banks and enter them into the database as well.

Electra is an app I am currently working on during my time with Talent Path. It is an app where artists in LA can build community by uploading files of their art and able to receive critiques and comments of their work. The goal of this app is it to be the “Stack Overflow” of the art scene in LA where people can get insight and advice from other local artists. I took on different hats for this app working on the frontend with React and server side with Node and Express.

Lost in Tokyo was made for me to be able to desgin and create a website about my expereincein Tokyo. I even made the main logo and wanted to start a blog.
